Meet the Owner

POPO Barbershop is proudly owned and managed by Diomedes Felices, a passionate barber and entrepreneur who believes that a good haircut builds confidence and character. With dedication, skill, and respect for every client, he turned his love for barbering into a trusted place for the community.

Our History

POPO Barbershop was established in 2012 with a simple goal: to provide quality haircuts at affordable prices. From a small setup and humble beginning, the shop slowly gained loyal customers through good service, friendly atmosphere, and consistent quality.

Our Success

Through hard work, patience, and trust from customers, POPO Barbershop grew into a well-known name in the area. The success of the barbershop is built on customer satisfaction, word of mouth, and continuous improvement in skills and service.

Challenges We Faced

Like any business, POPO Barbershop faced many challenges such as strong competition, limited resources, and difficult times. However, these challenges helped strengthen the business and motivated us to improve even more.

10 Years From Now

Ten years from now, we see POPO Barbershop as a bigger and stronger brand, offering more services, employing skilled barbers, and serving more customers while maintaining quality and trust.

Government Support

Government programs, training, and business support have helped POPO Barbershop improve operations and grow steadily. These supports inspired us to continue developing our services and contribute to the local economy.
